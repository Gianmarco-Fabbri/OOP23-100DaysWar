- Objective of the Game
The goal is to conquer as many tiles as possible within the 100 days available.

- Initial Setup
Each player is given a spawn tile. Every player starts with 0 troops. The player can perform various actions:

Buy soldiers for their army.
Buy towers, which can be either basic towers or advanced towers.
Upgrade towers and soldiers to become stronger.
Attack enemy troops using towers and units.
Take no action and skip the turn.
- Victory Conditions
There are two ways to win:

Conquer the enemy's spawn tile.
Conquer more tiles than the opponent.
